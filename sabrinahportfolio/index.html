About Me

Hello! My name is Sabrina Huffman, and I am currently in my second year of studying industrial design at OCAD University. I have a strong passion for human-centered design practices and take great pleasure in exploring innovative concepts. My work involves utilizing a diverse range of materials, fabrication techniques, and computer programs to bring my ideas to life. I am committed to creating thoughtful and effective design solutions that address real-world challenges.
